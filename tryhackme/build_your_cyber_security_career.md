**Module:** Build Your Cyber Security Career



**Rooms:** Security Principles | Careers in Cyber | Training Impact on Teams



**Theory:**

* *CIA* = Confidentiality - Integrity - Availability
* *DAD* = Disclosure - Alteration - Destruction/Denial



* *Trust but Verify* = constant verifying a trusted entity
* *Zero Trust* = every entity is considered adversarial until proven otherwise



* *Vulnerability* = exploitable weakness
* *Threat* = potential danger associated with weakness
* *Risk* = likelihood of vulnerability being exploited



* *Security Analyst* = constructing security measures to protect from attacks
* *Security Engineer* = developing and implementing security solutions to help prevent attacks
* *Incident Responder* = responding to attacks while they are still active
* *Digital Forensics Examiner* = collecting and analysis digital evidence for investigation
* *Malware Analyst* = analysing suspicious programs to learn more about their behaviour
* *Penetration Tester* = testing systems for security loopholes
* *Red Teamer* = testing focused on detection and response capabilities
